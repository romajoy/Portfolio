About Me

Born and raised in metro Detroit, I am a proud Michigander and Red Wings fan. Having worn many hats over the years (Salon Manager in Ann Arbor, Exec Admin in Amsterdam, and Librarian in Chicago) I enjoy taking on challenges, bringing order out of chaos, and managing teams with transparency.

Over my decade+ of management experience, I've developed a love for fostering a workplace that encourages belgonging and enablement. As I transition into the becoming a full-stack software engineer, I hope to incorporate what I've learned through my non-tradidtional background into my future jobs.

In my free time I enjoy reading dystopian novels, fostering cats and dogs, knitting sweaters and defending my fourth place spot in my euchre league.

If you'd like to reach out about possible jobs, freelance work, or want to talk fiber arts, please feel free to connect with me on any of the platforms below, or email me at joynormahouser@gmail.com!
